Extension Enabled
AI Shopping Assistant is active on Amazon websites

What This Extension Does

AI Shopping Assistant is a powerful Chrome extension that automates your Amazon shopping experience. It provides intelligent assistance through an AI-powered chat interface and can perform automated actions to streamline your purchasing process.

  • AI Chat Assistant
    Get personalized product recommendations and shopping advice through an intelligent chat interface
  • Automated Checkout
    Automate repetitive shopping tasks like clicking "Buy Now", selecting payment methods, and placing orders
  • Image Recognition
    Upload product images to get instant recommendations and find similar items on Amazon
  • Smart Recommendations
    Get AI-powered product suggestions based on your preferences, budget, and shopping history
Amazon Only: This extension is specifically designed to work on Amazon websites. It will only activate when you visit Amazon domains for your privacy and security.
